Common FAQs

What exactly is your degree?

I obtained a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, Management Information Assurance, focusing on data science and information assurance. Before graduating, I received a job offer for a Data Integration Project Management opportunity. I now work as a Business Analyst on an Implementation team migrating the third largest REIT in the world from its legacy billing system.

What do you like to do in your free time?

I grew up jumping from project to project, but my most noteworthy was at age twelve in 2008. I noticed a demand for cold drinks and snack food among construction workers in my developing neighborhood and knew bringing the product to them would enhance my returns. So I bought snack foods and beverages at wholesale stores and pushed a cart around my neighborhood, earning enough to buy a MacBook by the end of the first Summer. Fast-forward to today, and I usually bounce around coding websites, DJing bars and events, Training, and Day Trading.
